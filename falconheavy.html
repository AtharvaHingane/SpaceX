FALCON HEAVY

The Most Powerful Operational Rocket

Falcon Heavy generates more than 5 million pounds of thrust at liftoff, making it the most powerful rocket in operation today. Its triple-core design delivers unmatched lift capability for deep space missions.

SPECIFICATIONS

HEIGHT: 70 m

DIAMETER: 3.7 m

MASS: 1,420,788 kg

PAYLOAD TO LEO: 63,800 kg

PAYLOAD TO GTO: 26,700 kg

PAYLOAD TO MARS: 16,800 kg

BOOSTERS: 27 Merlin Engines
