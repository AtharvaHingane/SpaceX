FALCON 9

The World’s First Orbital-Class Reusable Rocket

Falcon 9 is a reusable, two-stage rocket designed and manufactured by SpaceX for the reliable and safe transport of people and payloads to Earth orbit and beyond.

Reusability allows SpaceX to refly the most expensive parts of the rocket, reducing the cost of space access.

SPECIFICATIONS

HEIGHT: 70 m

DIAMETER: 3.7 m

MASS: 549,054 kg

PAYLOAD TO LEO: 22,800 kg

PAYLOAD TO GTO: 8,300 kg

ENGINES: 9 × Merlin (First Stage)
