DRAGON

Delivering Crew & Cargo to the ISS

Dragon is the first private spacecraft capable of carrying humans to the International Space Station. It supports up to seven astronauts and delivers crucial scientific cargo safely to orbit.

Dragon remains a vital part of NASA’s Commercial Crew and Cargo programs.

SPECIFICATIONS

HEIGHT: 8.1 m

DIAMETER: 4 m

CREW CAPACITY: 7 people

PRESSURIZED VOLUME: 9.3 m³

TRUNK VOLUME: 37 m³

LAUNCH VEHICLE: Falcon 9
